# DoodleMaker

A lightweight, fun drawing app that inspires creativity through randomly generated shapes. Perfect for artists of any skill level!

## About

DoodleMaker generates random abstract shapes on colorful pastel backgrounds, giving you a creative starting point to transform into your own unique artwork. Whether you're an experienced artist looking for inspiration or a complete beginner wanting to practice, DoodleMaker makes drawing accessible and fun.

The app is designed to be simple, distraction-free, and encouraging. There's no pressure, no blank canvas anxiety—just playful shapes waiting to become something amazing.

## How to Use

### Getting Started

When you open DoodleMaker, you'll see random white shapes on a pastel-colored background. These shapes are your creative prompt! Use the drawing tools to turn them into whatever you imagine—a face, an animal, an abstract design, or anything else.

### Drawing Tools

Click the **menu button** (☰) in the top-right corner to show or hide the toolbar. Here's what each button does:

- **🔄 New Shape** - Generate a fresh set of random shapes with a new background color
- **✕ Clear** - Remove all your drawings while keeping the shapes
- **⬇ Download** - Open a preview showing before/after comparison, then download your finished artwork
- **⚙ Shape Settings** - Customize how many shapes appear (ovals, quads, rectangles, curves: 0-10 each)
- **🎨 Color Picker** - Cycle between three colors: black, white, or background color (acts as eraser)
- **📏 Brush Size** - Cycle through 5 brush sizes: 2px, 8px, 15px, 22px, 30px

### Mobile Devices

DoodleMaker works great on phones and tablets! Simply draw with your finger instead of a mouse.

**Important for mobile users:** When you download an image, it will save to your device's Downloads folder. On some mobile browsers, you may need to grant permission for downloads or long-press the image to save it manually.
